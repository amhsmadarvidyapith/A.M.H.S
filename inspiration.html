Aditya Nath, the former Chairman of Adwaita Mission group of Institutions, commanded the honour of translating the vision of Late Anand Shankar Madhavan into reality. Under his dynamic stewardship, the Vidyapith progressed leaps and bounds.

Late Aditya Nath served the society by opening up new avenues for children and youth. His disciplined life and indomitable will power will ever remain an illuminating inspiration for the whole Vidyapith family.
